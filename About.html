Katja Zibrek

MSc Psychology, PhD Computer Science

I currently hold a starting research position at the MimeTic team, Inria Rennes, France. I completed my undergraduate studies at the Department of Psychology, University of Ljubljana, Slovenia. My diploma focused on the behavioural and emotional responses to immersive environments, which led me to pursue a doctoral degree in computer science at Trinity Collge Dublin. In the doctoral thesis, I explored different ways of how appearance realism of virtual characters guides the perception and behaviour of the viewer.

Main areas of research: perception in virtual reality, social cognition, virtual characters, stylisation, 3D animation (motion capture).
