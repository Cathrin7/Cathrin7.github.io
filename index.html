Dr. Katja Zibrek

I am a MSCA-IF Postdoctoral Researcher at Inria Rennes, France. My main area of investigation is the perception of virtual humans in Virtual Reality. I use the methods from psychology and computer science to understand the impact of virtual human on users' subjective and behavioral response.
